## Cherry pie part 2

1. Filling ingredients

* cold cherry  - 350 gr

* sugar - 100 gr

* corn starch - 33 gr

2. Preparation of filling

mix all well

put on min stove until thickened.

3. Combine 

* roll out the dough

put into dish

then put the cherry filling 

put that beautiful pie into oven (170 C) for 45 minutes

finally done!
